How to Play

Welcome to the Animal Match Game! Your goal is to match pairs of tiles!

Instructions:

  1. Click on a tile to reveal the image.
  2. Click on another tile to find its matching pair.
  3. If the tiles match, they will stay face up. If they don't match, they will flip back.
  4. Keep flipping tiles and try to match all pairs.
  5. Your score increases by 10 points for each pair matched.
  6. You have 6 lives. Losing a pair costs you 1 life.
  7. If you lose all your lives, the game is over.
  8. Match all pairs to win!
